Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Huế Vietnamese Cuisine ("we", "us") operates huevietnamesecuisine.com. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Order details you provide at checkout (name, email, phone, items, pickup time).
  • Contact-form and email-signup submissions (name, email, phone, message).
  • Basic, aggregated analytics about site visits if you accept analytics cookies.

Payments

Card and wallet payments are processed by our payment providers (Stripe and PayPal). We do not store full card numbers on our servers.

How we use it

  • To prepare and fulfill your pickup orders and respond to your messages.
  • To send the coupon or freebie you requested, and occasional specials (you can opt out anytime).
  • To understand site traffic and improve the experience.

Sharing

We share data only with service providers that help us run the site and orders (for example payment, email, and hosting providers). We do not sell your personal information.

Your choices

You can decline analytics cookies in the banner, unsubscribe from emails using the link in any message, or contact us to access or delete your information.

AI & Transparency

Last updated: June 2026

We are a small family restaurant. To keep our prices low and serve you faster, this website was designed and built with the help of generative AI tools (including Anthropic’s Claude). We believe in being open about that.

Images & illustrations

Some visuals on this site are simple icons or may be AI-generated or AI-enhanced for illustration only. The actual ingredients, portion sizes, and appearance of our food follow our real recipes and may vary with fresh, seasonal ingredients. Any food image is an artistic representation, not a guarantee of exact appearance. For a true look at our daily kitchen, see our latest social posts or visit us.

Real people, real food

AI helps power our website and design. Everything that matters — cooking, food quality, customer service, and order pickup — is handled by real people in our kitchen. We do not use AI to depict real, identifiable individuals without their consent.

Accuracy

Menu items, prices, and hours are kept as accurate as we can. If anything looks off, please tell us and we will correct it.
